Privacy Policy for Boba Bliss

Last updated: June 27, 2025

Introduction

Boba Bliss ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Visit Data: Location names, addresses, ratings, prices, notes, and photos you add to your boba tea visits
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app usage statistics
  • Location Data: Precise location data when you use the location picker feature (only with your permission)

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: All your visit data is stored locally on your device
  • Google Drive Sync: If you enable Google Drive sync, your data is backed up to your personal Google Drive account
  • No Cloud Database: We do not store your personal data on our servers

How We Use Your Information

Primary Functions

  • Visit Tracking: Store and display your boba tea visit history
  • Location Services: Help you find and save boba tea shop locations
  • Data Backup: Provide Google Drive sync functionality for data backup and restoration

Analytics

  • App Performance: Monitor app crashes and performance issues to improve user experience
  • Usage Statistics: Analyze feature usage to enhance app functionality

Third-Party Services

Google Services

  • Google Maps: Used for location picking and map display
  • Google Drive: Used for data backup and sync (only when enabled by user)
  • Google Analytics: Used for app performance monitoring
Data Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared with Google services when you explicitly enable features.

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

  • Delete Data: You can delete individual visits or all data through the app
  • Export Data: Export your data through the app's backup feature
  • Disable Sync: Turn off Google Drive sync at any time

Permissions

  • Location: You can deny location permissions and still use the app
  • Camera: Required only for taking photos of your drinks
  • Storage: Required for saving photos and app data

Data Security

Protection Measures

  • Local Encryption: Data stored locally is protected by device security
  • Secure Transmission: All data transmitted to Google services uses HTTPS
  • No Server Storage: We don't store your data on our servers

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Google Drive: Follows Google's data retention policies
  • Analytics: Aggregated data retained for app improvement

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date

Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@bobabliss.app
  • App Support: Through the app's settings page

Legal Basis

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Google Play Store requirements

Consent

By using Boba Bliss, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.
